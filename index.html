about me

Hello! My name is Czy, but you can call me "Ven", "Czy", "Venczy", or "ดาว" which means "star". I’ve been editing since 2018. I started when the sasswars videos were famous. I learned and discovered editing through TikTok. The first editing software I used was "Kinemaster". It was a great experience, and now I am using "After Effects 2025".

Why do I have my Thai Nickname?

It started in 2020 when Thai Series became really famous. And upon watching some of them, I found the Thai language cute and challenging. So I challenged myself to learn Thai too! One of the reasons why I’m learning it is because I’m planning to study at Chulalongkorn University.

What are the styles you are doing?

My main editing style is "Candy Style", and I’m currently working on "Typography" as well. If you’d ask me which one is harder to learn and create, I’d say "Candy Style". It has so many details—some are meaningful and can barely be noticed, maybe just 3 frames long haha! But learning it from the start is actually fun. It tests your creativity and helps you discover more about editing.

Where do you get your resources?

I get my resources sometimes just from "Google", and also from other editors. I also buy project files from fellow editors, so I can use and learn from the resources they used too. Buying their project files helps me understand their style and how they use graphs.
